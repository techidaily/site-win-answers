### Fix 1: Make sure your PC meets the minimum requirements

| Operating System | Windows 10, 8 or 7 (some functionality not supported on Windows 7 and 8) |

| GPU              | NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 or AMD Radeon HD 7870 or equivalent DX11 GPU      |

3. Click**Get Updates** . Then all your applications should update (including Minecraft Dungeons).
