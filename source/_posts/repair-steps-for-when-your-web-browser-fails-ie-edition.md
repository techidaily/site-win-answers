 1) Press the **Power button** on the front panel to turn off your PlayStation 4\. The power indicator will **blink** for a few moments before turning off.

 2) After turning off your PS4, press and hold the **Power button** , then release it after you hear two **beeps** : one when you press at first, and the other in seven seconds later.

 4) You will enter the **Safe Mode** and see 8 options. Select **5\. Rebuild Database** . Then wait for it to complete.
