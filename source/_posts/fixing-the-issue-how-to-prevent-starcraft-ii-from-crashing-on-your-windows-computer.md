1. Press the**Windows logo key + I** together to open the**Windows Settings** .
2. Click**Apps** .  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/app2.jpg)

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/app.jpg)
