1. Run Steam.
2. In the**Library** section, right-click on BioShock 2 Remastered and click**Properties** .
3. Under the**LOCAL FILES** tab, click**BROWSE LOCAL FILES** to the game folder.

4. Open file “Bioshock2SP.ini”
