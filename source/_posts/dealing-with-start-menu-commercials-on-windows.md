##  The Windows 11 Start Menu Now Has Ads

 Ads in the Windows start menu are disguised as app 'recommendations'. Apps you have never used will appear in the menu, taking space away from the apps you actually use, and potentially luring your young friends or family members into clicking on them and purchasing them through the [Windows Store](https://some-skills.techidaily.com/new-ultimate-listings-for-3d-type-creation/).

##  Steps to Disable Ads in the Windows 11 Start Menu

 Thankfully, disabling these ads is easy, just follow these steps:

1. Open the Windows Settings App from the Start menu or by pressing Windows+i.
2. Click on "Personalization" in the sidebar.
3. Click on "Start" on the Personalization screen.
4. Un-check "Show recommendations for tips, shortcuts, new apps, and more."
