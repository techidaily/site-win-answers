 It has been proved by many users that some antivirus software like Bitdefender is the reason for the Rainbow Six Siege won‚Äôt launch issue. Antivirus software forbidden the game and cause the problem.

 If it works, add Rainbow Six Siege into your antivirus software exception list to fix the problem.

 For Bitdefender users, you can add Rainbow Six Siege into the exception list to avoid the won‚Äôt launch issue.  
 Go to**Protection** \>**Advanced Threat Defence** \>**Manage Exceptions.**
 Then add rainbowsix.exe and rainbowsix\_vulkan.exe
